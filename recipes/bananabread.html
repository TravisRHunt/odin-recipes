Easy Banana Bread

Description

Quick and easy banana bread

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps
